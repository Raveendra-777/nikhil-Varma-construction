PROFILE

Defining Construction Excellence

Established in 2020, Nikhil Varma Constructions is a Bangalore-based firm driven by a passion for building strong, lasting structures with integrity and precision. We are committed to delivering Quality construction solutions that reflect our core values of Trust, Transparency & Time Bound. With a focus on technical accuracy, timely execution, and customer satisfaction, we approach every project as an opportunity to create something meaningful. Our steady growth & strong client relationships stand as a testament to the reliability, consistency & professionalism we bring to the construction industry.

CIVIL CONSTRUCTION & DEVELOPMENT

At Nikhil Varma Constructions, we specialize in delivering robust and reliable civil construction and development solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each project. From site preparation to structural execution, we handle every aspect with precision, quality, and safety in mind. Our team ensures that each structure is built to last, using the best materials, techniques, and industry practices to bring architectural visions to life while adhering to timelines and regulatory standards.


RCC PRECAST COMPOUND WALLS


We provide durable and cost-effective walls that ensure quick installation & long-lasting performance. Designed for strength, security & stability, our precast solutions offer excellent protection and a clean finish for residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

STRUCTURE REPAIRS


We offer reliable solution to restore the integrity and safety of damaged or aging buildings. Using proven techniques and quality materials, we address cracks, deterioration, and structural weaknesses to extend the life and strength of your construction.

WATERPROOFING


Our waterproofing solutions are designed to protect structures from water damage, leakage, and dampness. We use high-quality materials and proven methods to ensure long-lasting protection for roofs, basements, walls, and terraces‚Äîenhancing the durability and safety of every building.

MS STRUCTURAL WORKS


We deliver precise and robust MS (Mild Steel) structural works for various construction needs, including frames, trusses, platforms, and support structures. Our fabrication and installation services ensure strength, stability, and seamless integration into your building design.

STP WORKS


We provide complete STP (Sewage Treatment Plant) works, including earthwork, structural construction, and installation support. Our solutions are designed to meet environmental standards, ensuring efficient wastewater management and sustainable infrastructure for residential and commercial projects.

CONCRETE FLOORING & FINISHING


We offer high-quality concrete flooring with smooth and durable finishing, suitable for both industrial and residential applications. Our services include precise leveling, IPS and floater finishes, ensuring a clean, strong, and long-lasting surface with minimal maintenance.

VILLA & COMMERCIAL CONSTRUCTION

We specialize in the construction of premium villas and commercial spaces with a focus on quality, functionality, and aesthetic appeal. From concept to completion, we ensure structurally sound, well-designed buildings that meet client expectations and comply with all regulatory standards..
